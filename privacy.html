Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

Who we are

Social Chicago Collective ("SCC", "we", "us") is a culture systems studio based in Chicago, IL. Our website is socialchicagocollective.com.

What we collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide through our inquiry forms:

  • Name and email address
  • Venue or artist name
  • Social media handles and website URLs
  • Any additional context you provide in form fields

We also collect standard web analytics data (pages visited, referral source, device type) through cookies and analytics tools to understand how visitors use our site.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide our services
  • To process payments through Stripe (our payment processor)
  • To improve our website and service offerings
  • To send follow-up communications related to your inquiry

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Payment processing

Payments are processed securely through Stripe. We do not store credit card numbers or payment details on our servers. Stripe's privacy policy governs the handling of your payment information.

Form submissions

Form data submitted through our website is processed by Netlify Forms. Submissions are stored securely and accessible only to SCC team members.

Cookies

We use analytics cookies to understand site traffic and visitor behavior. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing us at alex@socialchicagocollective.com.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at alex@socialchicagocollective.com.
