Questions

Before you buy.

Who is this for?

Artists with real-world signal — taste, story, stage presence, a sound — who can't figure out why their online presence isn't connecting. You don't need to be big. You need to have something real to work with.

What do I need to send you?

Your links (Instagram, TikTok, Spotify, website) and any live performance clips you have. A quick note about what you're building right now — release, tour, offer, goals. That's it. The intake form is short.

What if I don't have much content yet?

That's actually a good time to do this. The audit tells you what to build before you build it wrong for another six months. We can read your signal from performance footage, interviews, and what you've posted — even if it's not much.

Is this a consultation call?

No. The primary deliverable is asynchronous — a recorded Loom and a PDF you can watch and read on your own time and share with your team. The follow-up call is for questions after you've had time to process it.

What happens after the audit?

Nothing required. You get the deliverable and can take it wherever. If you want to go further — into a full World System Build or an ongoing retainer — that's available, and the audit price applies toward it. But there's no push.

What's the refund policy?

If we haven't started work yet (before you've submitted your intake form), full refund, no questions. Once we've started the audit, we'll finish it — but we don't issue refunds after work has begun.

Can I ask a question before buying?

Yes. Email us at hello@socialchicagocollective.com and we'll reply within 24 hours. Or go to the Artist Strategy page to learn more about the full scope first.
