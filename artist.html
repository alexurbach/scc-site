FAQ

Common questions.

What exactly is an "Artist World Audit"?

A recorded Loom showing the gap between what you communicate in person vs. what your online presence communicates — plus a first-pass blueprint covering positioning, visual direction, and formats. Delivered as a PDF you can share with your team.

Who is this for?

Artists with real signal — taste, story, performance energy — who want a coherent world that scales on mobile (IG / TikTok / Spotify / YouTube) and supports releases, merch, and offers.

How is this different from hiring a social media manager?

A social media manager runs your account. We build the system that makes your account worth running. Positioning, visual identity, format engineering — the foundation that makes every post, reel, and campaign feel like one cohesive world instead of random content.

Do I need a certain follower count?

No. Follower count is irrelevant. What matters is that you have real-world signal — a sound, a stage presence, a story, a taste level that people respond to. We've worked with emerging artists and established ones. The system works at any scale.

Do you run day-to-day posting?

Only on retainer. The core value is building the system — then we iterate with performance feedback if you want ongoing support. Most clients run execution themselves after the build.

What do you need from me to get started?

Links + a short note about what you're building right now. If you have live performance clips, send those — your strongest source signal is usually in motion.

How long does it take?

Audit delivered within 5 business days. The full World System Build runs 2-4 weeks depending on scope and revision cycles.

What's the refund policy?

If we haven't started work yet (before you've submitted your intake form), full refund, no questions. Once we've started the audit, we'll finish it — but we don't issue refunds after work has begun.
